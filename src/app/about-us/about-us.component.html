About the IVF cost in Pakistan

(In Vitro Fertilization) IVF cost in Pakistan can vary widely depending on several factors, including the clinic, the specific services provided, the expertise of the medical professionals, and the region in Pakistan. IVF is a complex medical procedure that involves various steps, including ovarian stimulation, egg retrieval, fertilization, embryo culture, embryo transfer, etc. You should contact the GENOVA fertility centre for accurate and up-to-date information on IVF costs.
